Why Your Dog Barks at Night

Many dogs bark more at night due to boredom, anxiety, or increased alertness when the house becomes quiet.

Yelling or punishment often makes it worse. A more effective approach is redirecting your dog’s energy using short brain-training routines.


See the Brain Training Method

Tip: Consistency beats intensity. Small daily mental challenges can calm many dogs over time.
