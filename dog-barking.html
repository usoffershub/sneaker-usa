Why Dogs Bark at Night

Many dogs bark more at night due to boredom, anxiety, or extra “alert mode” when the house is quiet.

Yelling or punishment often makes it worse. A simple approach is to redirect the dog’s energy with short brain-training games and routines.

See the Brain Training Method

Tip: Consistency beats intensity. Small daily mental challenges can calm many dogs over time.
